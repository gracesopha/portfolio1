an introduction

T hank you for taking the time to visit! My name is Grace Sopha, an IT Specialist with a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology and Management, based in Chicago. Ever since I was a little girl, I've always been curious of computers and technology. Stemming from a family of computer scientists, it was an advantageous feat for me. I often found myself sneaking into my dad's gear to fiddle with the latest gadgets and gizmos - only imagining I was a super-scientist engineering the next big device. To think that 20 years later I would become one!

Well, maybe not a super-scientist, but you get the jist. In hindsight, I have dedicated most of my life to studying technology whether I knew it or not. However, throughout my formal education, I have explored a variety of tastes that IT has to offer. This website serves as the hub for all of my thoughts regarding my work and projects! Once again, thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy your stay!
