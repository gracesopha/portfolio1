INFRASTRUCTURE DESIGN & SYSTEM MANAGEMENT

T he final project for Introduction to Information Technology as a Profession. I led a group of five to create an extensive business proposal that encourages rainwater supply, an 80-year-old family-owned company, to update their IT infrastructure to a modern framework. Considering their assets, needs, and goals for the future, a recommendation to break into the e-commerce atmosphere was made, along with detailed steps to achieve this movement. Additionally, a budget sheet is included to supplement the choices made by our team of IT System Managers, including one-time and recurring purchases.
