DESIGN & ENTREPRENEURSHIP

H ow do you reinvent one of the world's most popular sports for one of the world's most reputable companies in a world that is immersed in technology? For our inter-professional design and entrepreneurship project, we teamed up with Wilson Sporting Goods to research how we can bring golf to an e-commerce environment. Throughout the semester, our team explored various examples of product customization and personalization successfully demonstrated in other industries, with a special focus on in-person retail supplanted by online versions. As the Covid pandemic changed the way we live our daily lives, it became a necessity for corporations to extend their services to a virtual landscape. As over 80% of Wilson's golf clubs are customized and fitted to their players, we theorized an interchangeable club that can be used solely for fittings, diminishing the need for in-person appointments, saving not only time but money for both parties.
