DESIGN & ENTREPRENEURSHIP

H ow do you reinvent one of the world's most popular sports for one of the world's most reputable companies in a world that is immersed in technology? For our inter-professional design and entrepreneurship project, we teamed up with Wilson Sporting Goods to research how we can bring golf to an e-commerce environment. As the Covid pandemic changed the way we live our daily lives, it became a necessity for corporations to extend their services to a virtual landscape.

As most, if not all, golf clubs are customized and fitted to their players, we theorized an interchangeable club that can be used solely for fittings, diminishing the need for in-person appointments, saving not only time but money for both parties.
