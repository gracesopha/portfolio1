tutorme

ANDROIDSTUDIO, JAVA, MYSQL

As university students, we are constantly relying on the knowledge of others. This necessity for our demographic inspired my inter-professional team project to create tutorMe - an app that allows university students looking to connect with a tutor or even become one! Our group, consisting of two ITM majors and three CS majors, programmed our mobile app using AndroidStudio, Java and MySQL to bring tutors and students together on their own time and terms! During the semester of the global product development course, we built the foundation of our application through the agile methodology. Rotating positions as Scrum Lead, Developer, Database Manager, UI/UX Designer, etc. for each sprint, we all individually got to expand our skills in these areas.
